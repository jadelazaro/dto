## Quick Access Guide

### To Login:
1. **Option A**: Click "Login" in header (top-right)
2. **Option B**: Scroll down and click "Admin Login" in Contact section
3. Enter: username `admin`, password `admin123`
4. Click "Login"

### To Logout:
1. **Option A**: Click "Logout" in header (top-right)
2. **Option B**: Scroll down and click "Logout" in Admin Access box
3. Confirm in the modal that appears
4. Click "Sign Out"

### To Find Contact Info:
- Scroll to Contact & Authentication section
- Contact info on left side:
  - Phone
  - Email
  - Address
